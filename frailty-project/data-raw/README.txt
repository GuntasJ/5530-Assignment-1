Frailty is physical weakness; lack of health or strength. Reduced grip strength in females
correlated with higher frailty scores and vice versa. Yes means the subject has frailty. No means they do not. 

Hand grip strength can be quantified by measuring the amount of static force that the hand can squeeze around a dynamometer. The
force has most commonly been measured in kilograms and pounds.
