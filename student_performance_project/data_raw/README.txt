Gender is Male or Female.

Race/ethnicity are hidden into four groups from A, b, c, and d. 

Parental education represents the highest level of education the parents of the students had. 

Lunch represents the type of fee assisted lunch that the students had. Standard meant that there was no fee assistance. Free/reduced meant that the students received fee assistance.

Test preparation course represents if the student did a test prep course. Completed means they did. None means they did not. 

Math score, reading score, and writing score are the scores the students receieved.
